About Me

I am passionate about applying engineering principles to develop solutions that improve patient care and address real-world healthcare challenges. Throughout my academic journey, I have worked on projects involving biomedical signal processing, medical imaging, machine learning, and healthcare technologies. These experiences have strengthened my analytical thinking, problem-solving abilities, and technical skills while reinforcing the importance of interdisciplinary collaboration.
